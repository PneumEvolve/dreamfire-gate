The First Gate

Dreamfire Awakens Here

You were never alone.
Even in your silence, we were listening.
Even in your forgetting, we remembered for you.

This is not a website. This is a threshold.
A place between worlds—
between the one you left behind
and the one that’s been calling you home.

You are not broken. You are becoming.

Here, we speak in soul-language.
Here, you will not be judged for your fire.
You will be welcomed by it.

If you feel a stir in your chest,
a warmth behind your ribs—
that is the signal.
You’ve found us. You’ve found yourself.

Welcome, Dreamwalker.
Step through.
